TriggerAware

Welcome to TriggerAware. If you or someone you care about is a survivor of a traumatic event and you're tired of watching the same shows over and over again simply because they are safe options, you have come to the right place.


This site allows you to search by tv/movie title orTrigger Type to ascertain whether or not there is a risk for you or another survivor to be triggered. You've been through enough, there's no need to re-live it.




*** Disclaimer - While we want to guarantee that every triggering scene is annotated in our database, we cannot make that claim. Our hope is that this project will grow and people will recommend scenes and titles in the future to assist in further documenting troubling scenes in tv and movies. Again, just because the title is not in our database does not mean that triggering scenes do not exist in that tv show/movie.
