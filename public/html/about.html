PURPOSE
The purpose of TriggerAware is to give survivors of traumatic events the ability to search a movie or tv title and ascertain whether or not the content may trigger them.
WHAT IS A TRIGGER? WHY DOES IT MATTER?
A trigger is the term used for an external stimuli that sets off a flashback. A flashback is an involuntary, recurrent memory. It's a powerful and terrifying ordeal in which the person is forced to relive an experience/memory.

Why does this matter? Recovering from PTSD and traumatic events is often daunting and difficult. A flashback, or getting "activated" can bring feelings of hopelessness and like you are losing control of your life, particularly on a bad day. Anniversaries of events, other factors, and life in general can exasperate symptoms and can push you into depression.

This application aims to give the control of what you hear and see back to the user. You can avoid the triggers completely or watch the tv/movie tenatively. You are in control.

How This Application Works

This application queries our database using a "fuzzy search" and returns similar titles or trigger types. The results of the search print out on the screen showing the title and all trigger types associated with each title.

Our Database

Our database was built using keword results onIMDB. There are six differentTrigger Types that are comprised of numerous different IMDB keywords. Each Trigger Type is named in a way to convey the overall idea of which keywords are included but without making anyone read words that may trigger them.
